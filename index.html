About

Hello, I'm Braydon, a Junior IT Support Officer currently working with Techquity in North Hobart.


In 2024, I completed my Certificate III in Information Technology at TasTAFE and have partially completed my Certificate IV in Information Technology (Networking), having successfully finished nine units. I decided to withdraw from the course in order to focus on gaining more practical, hands-on experience in the industry.


My core interests lie in web development, system administration, networking, and cybersecurity. I enjoy building websites, designing and simulating networks, troubleshooting technical issues, and managing IT services. I’m proficient in HTML and CSS, with a solid understanding of JavaScript and experience using Sass as a pre-processor for styling. In addition to my web development skills, I have experience using PowerShell for system administration and Python for scripting.


Currently, I’m expanding my knowledge in a range of technologies, including Git and GitHub for version control, as well as Microsoft Azure and Microsoft 365 to expand my knowledge of modern IT infrastructures. I'm also strengthening my skills in networking and cybersecurity to build a well-rounded IT foundation.


I’m always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow in the IT industry. If you have any questions or would like to get in touch, feel free to contact me using the form below.
